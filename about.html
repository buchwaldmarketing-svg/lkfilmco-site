The Filmmaker

Lee Kounas.
Dallas wedding filmmaker.

Marketing grad from UT Dallas. I picked up a camera, started experimenting, and never really stopped. What I kept coming back to was how much more video holds than a photo — you can hear the voice, see the way someone moves, feel the actual energy of a moment.

I film my own kids constantly because I know exactly what those moments are worth down the road. That's the same reason I show up locked in on your wedding day.

Working with me feels natural — and fun. I'll crack a joke, put you at ease, and give you a small prompt if I need something specific. But I'm never over-directing or making things stiff. I read the room, move with your day, and work closely with your photo team so nothing falls through the cracks.

Outside of filming: my wife and I are big on travel, the outdoors, and soccer with the kids. And coffee — specifically an espresso over ice.

Filming since 2020. Based in Dallas. Available wherever your story takes you.
