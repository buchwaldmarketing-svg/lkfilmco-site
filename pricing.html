Your films are delivered through a private online gallery — stream anytime, download in full quality, share with family. These are yours forever.
FAQ

Common questions.

What do we get with every package?
Every couple gets a 6–8 minute cinematic highlight film and a 60-second social teaser — plus professional color grading, two camera setup, lighting, and audio. The difference between packages comes down to hours, ceremony/speech edits, and whether you want a second shooter.
Do you work with a second shooter?
I fly solo on most weddings — it keeps the energy calm and the footage consistent. The Complete Day package includes a second shooter for dual-angle ceremony coverage and more ground covered during prep.
What's your filming style?
Cinematic documentary. I don't pose or direct — I stay in the background and capture what's real. Couples tell me it felt like I wasn't even there. That's the goal.
Do you travel?
Anywhere within one hour of downtown Dallas is included at no extra cost. Beyond that, we'll figure out travel together — I keep it simple and fair.
What about payment?
A 30% retainer locks in your date. The remaining balance is due two weeks before the wedding. I accept Zelle, Venmo, or cash — no credit card fees, no payment portals.
Can we customize a package?
Absolutely. These packages are a starting point. If you want to swap something out or add on, just let me know — I'll put together something that fits.
When should we book?
As soon as you're ready. I only take a limited number of weddings per year so I can give each one the attention it deserves. Fall and spring dates go fast in Texas.
