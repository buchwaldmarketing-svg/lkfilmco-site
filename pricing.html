What to Expect

From inquiry to delivery.

01
Reach out
Send me a message with your date, venue, and anything you want me to know about your day. I'll get back to you within a few hours.
02
Let's connect
We'll chat over text or email, whatever's easiest for you. I want to hear about your day, your vibe, and what matters most to you. No sales pitch, just a real conversation.
03
Lock in your date
Once you're ready, I'll send over a simple contract and invoice. A 30% retainer secures your date. The rest is due two weeks before the wedding. Payment via Zelle, Venmo, or cash.
04
Pre-wedding planning
About a month out, we'll go over your timeline together. I'll coordinate with your planner and photographer so the day runs smooth. No surprises.
05
Your wedding day
This is where I do my thing. I show up early, connect with you, crack a joke to break the tension, and then capture everything: the first look nerves, the ugly-cry toasts, the dance floor chaos. I'm present but never in the way. You'll feel comfortable the whole time.
06
Post-production
I take my time in the edit: color grading, audio mixing, music selection. Your film is built from scratch, not a template. Expect 12–16 weeks for delivery. If you booked a social teaser, that comes first within 2–3 weeks.
07
Delivery
Your films are delivered through a private online gallery: stream anytime, download in full quality, share with family. These are yours forever.
FAQ

Common questions.

What do we get with every package?
Every couple gets a cinematic highlight film, a 60-second social teaser, a speeches & toasts edit, and drone coverage. Plus professional color grading, two camera setup, lighting, and audio. The difference between packages comes down to hours of coverage, and the longer the day, the longer your highlight film.
Do you work with a second shooter?
I fly solo on most weddings. Keeps the energy calm and the footage consistent. A second shooter is available as an add-on for dual-angle ceremony coverage and more ground covered during prep.
What's your filming style?
Cinematic documentary. I keep things natural. I might give you a small prompt here and there, but nothing stiff or forced. Couples tell me I made them feel comfortable and they barely noticed the camera. That's the goal.
Do you travel?
Anywhere within one hour of downtown Dallas is included at no extra cost. Beyond that, we'll figure out travel together. I keep it simple and fair.
What about payment?
A 30% retainer locks in your date. The remaining balance is due two weeks before the wedding. I accept Zelle, Venmo, or cash. No credit card fees, no payment portals.
Can we customize a package?
Absolutely. These packages are a starting point. If you want to swap something out or add on, just let me know. I'll put together something that fits.
When should we book?
As soon as you're ready. I only take a limited number of weddings per year so I can give each one the attention it deserves. Fall and spring dates go fast in Texas.
