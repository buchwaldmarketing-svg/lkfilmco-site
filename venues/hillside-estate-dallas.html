About This Venue

Filming at The Hillside Estate

The Hillside Estate is one of the most photogenic venues in Dallas. There's indoor/outdoor flexibility with natural light everywhere you look. The grounds give you real options for ceremony and portraits — you're not locked into one spot.

Whether it's a smaller, intimate wedding or a larger celebration, the space works. The architecture photographs well, the landscaping adds texture without competing with the couple, and the lighting throughout the day is consistently good.

I've filmed here multiple times and the light never disappoints. It's one of those venues where the footage practically edits itself.
